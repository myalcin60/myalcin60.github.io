About me

Musa Yalcin is an educator, experienced teacher, and QA tester.

He worked as a teacher and administrator at the private Yamanlar educational institution in Turkey. In 2021, he relocated to France, where he pursued French language education at Aix-Marseille University. Additionally, he received remote training in Software Development Engineering in Test from Clarusway Education Center in the United States.

Musa holds a bachelor's degree in Mathematics from Afyon Kocatepe University, along with a master's degree in Mathematics Teaching. He is proficient in English at the A2 level and in French at the B1 level. His native language is Turkish.

Currently, Musa is actively working towards advancing his career as a QA tester.
